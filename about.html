Well, who am I?

I am a highly driven creative who is always ready to get stuck in with anything challenging that enters my radar. I place heavy importance on community and enjoy working with great teams to get the best out of each and every member. I pride myself on the genuine impact my projects have, and the connections people make as a result of them.

You can often find me teaching creative workshops, giving critical feedback to those who ask, or hunched over my computer switching between too many Adobe programs. I am a key believer in the medium is the message, so my outcomes take many forms. I have created everything from prints, to packaging, to podcast, to film. I truly embody a ‘how hard can it be?’ mentality.

With a dedication to good communication and time management, I thrive in a bustling environment which involves teamwork as well as individual work. I am always looking for ways to learn something new, and never shy away from a challenge.
